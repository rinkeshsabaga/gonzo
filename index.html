ABOUT
GONZO

Gonzo is not just a street diner. It’s a state of mind.

A walking street-style hangout where bold Asian street grubs meet pop-culture gulps, Gonzo turns up the volume on what a street food hangout should be: loud, messy, delicious, and full of surprises.

Think neon signs, industrial corners, custom mocktails, and flavour bombs from Bangkok to Busan. Every night feels like a group trip that spirals - in the best way possible.

No velvet ropes. No stuck-up menus. Just uninhibited energy, cult flavours, and a space that feels like nowhere else.

The streets called. We answered.

MISSION

TO SERVE UNAPOLOGETICALLY BOLD FOOD AND DRINKS INSPIRED BY ASIAN STREET CULTURE, IN A SPACE THAT FEELS LIKE A POP-CULTURE PLAYGROUND, FREE, CHAOTIC, AND BUILT FOR GOOD TIMES.

VISION

TO BECOME INDIA’S MOST ICONIC STREET-STYLE DINER - BLENDING FOOD, FLAVOUR, AND CULTURAL ENERGY INTO A LIFESTYLE MOVEMENT THAT REDEFINES HOW THIS GENERATION EATS, GULPS, AND UNWINDS.

LIVE EXPERIENCES

EDIBLE CHAOS & SOUNDS OF THE STREETS

THE LAUNCH

HOT SAUCE CLUB

Where things get spicy, messy, and gloriously unhinged. From sumo suits at sundowners to slurping ramen in sequins—we don’t throw events, we throw edible chaos. The evening came alive with sumo wrestling, custom sauce challenges, and interactive chaos that got people to play and connect.

THEMED NIGHT

TOKYO DRIFT

Tokyo Drift transformed Gonzo into a JDM-inspired playground with live car showcases, neon lights, themed craft sodas, and nonstop beats. The energy, the crowd, and the chaos turned this night into one of Gonzo’s most iconic and raw cinematic atmospheric events.

PRIVATE WORKSHOP

WRAP IT UP

We curated a private dim-sum-making workshop with Shradha Sahney (BingeNotCringe), bringing together a handpicked circle of 10-12 creators. One long table, flour-covered hands, shared conversations, and creators learning, folding, laughing, and shooting together.

KO BY
GONZO

KO is Gonzo’s new flavour-forward dining lounge — inspired by “knocked out” and built for nights with a little more punch. It carries the same Gonzo energy, but elevated: cleaner lines, stronger flavours, deeper profiles, and a more refined, sensory-centric cuisine experience.

Think bold signature mocktails, spicy pairings, artisan yuzu and tea rituals, and custom plates designed to hit without the hype. Premium, but still playful. Street, but sharper.
