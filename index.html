ABOUT
GONZO

Gonzo is not just a street diner. It’s a state of mind.

A walking street-style hangout where bold Asian street grubs meet pop-culture gulps, Gonzo turns up the volume on what a street food hangout should be: loud, messy, delicious, and full of surprises.

Think neon signs, industrial corners, custom mocktails, and flavour bombs from Bangkok to Busan. Every night feels like a group trip that spirals - in the best way possible.

No velvet ropes. No stuck-up menus. Just uninhibited energy, cult flavours, and a space that feels like nowhere else.

The streets called. We answered.

View Menu

THE LEGEND OF DR. GONZO

Every great trip needs a guide who’s a little unhinged. Ours is Dr. Gonzo — part myth, part mascot, all chaos. He was born from the gonzo spirit: raw, first-person, no-filter, throw-yourself-into-the-madness.

Dr. Gonzo doesn’t do velvet ropes or stuck-up menus. He prescribes bold Asian street grubs, pop-culture gulps, and the kind of energy you can’t fake. The streets called. Dr. Gonzo answered — and built a diner around the diagnosis: more flavour, more noise, more life.

EVENTS & COMMUNITY

EDIBLE CHAOS & SOUNDS OF THE STREETS

THEMED NIGHT

TOKYO DRIFT

Tokyo Drift transformed Gonzo into a JDM-inspired playground with live car showcases, neon lights, themed craft sodas, and nonstop beats. The energy, the crowd, and the chaos turned this night into one of Gonzo’s most iconic and raw cinematic events.

THE LAUNCH

HOT SAUCE CLUB

Where things get spicy, messy, and gloriously unhinged. From sumo suits at sundowners to slurping ramen in sequins — we don’t throw events, we throw edible chaos. Sumo wrestling, custom sauce challenges, and interactive chaos that got people to play and connect.
