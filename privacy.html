Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. The Vibe (Introduction)

Welcome to Gonzo Asian ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (gonzoasian.in) or visit our diner in Gurugram. We take your privacy as seriously as our flavor profiles.

2. What We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Personal Info: Name, email address, phone number, and reservation details when you book a table or contact us.
  • Spicy Details: Any feedback, inquiries, or "spicy" messages you send through our contact form.
  • Digital Footprint: IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our site (via cookies and similar tracking tech).

3. How We Use It

We don't sell your data. We use it to keep the chaos organized:

  • To process reservations and ensure your table is ready.
  • To send you booking confirmations or event updates.
  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback.
  • To improve our website experience and analyze traffic patterns.

4. Sharing the Sauce

We only share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you (like reservation platforms), as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. But remember, the internet isn't 100% secure—even if our firewall is as tough as our wok heat.
